About

I am a PhD Candidate at Queensland University of Technology in the Faculty of Engineering, School of Mechanical, Medical and Process Engineering. My passions include women's health, use of new and evolving techniques, and sharing my research with others. My research focuses on characterising the interactions between Triple-Negative Breast Cancer and vascular structures at local and metastatic sites. I aim to create a novel three-dimensional, microfluidic, humanized model for anti-angiogeneic and anti-metastatic drug testing.
